About Pickleball Hub

Welcome to Pickleball Hub

Located in Purok 3, Baluntay, Alabel, Pickleball Hub is a community-driven destination built for everyone who loves the game. Whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or sharpening your competitive skills, our courts provide the perfect place to play.

Our goal is simple! Create a welcoming environment where friends, families, and athletes can enjoy pickleball while building an active and supportive community.
